Natural Disaster Trends 1900-2021

A prompt on Earth.Org inspired me to create this dashboard, which looks at trends in natural disasters. Specifically, I looked at the frequency and magnitude of floods, cyclones, earthquakes, and wildfires from 1900-2021.

Dataset:

I found the dataset I needed on Kaggle: ALL NATURAL DISASTERS 1900-2021 / EOSDIS

Cleaning and Enhancing:

I used Pandas to begin cleaning the data, using VSCode via Jupyter Notebooks. Once I got the data to a managable size, I finished cleaning and did my enhancing in Google Sheets.

Analysis:

I imported my csv to Tableau and used basic visualizations to determine the scope of my project before creating my dashboard.
