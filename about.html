What do playing music, planning tours, and writing code have in common?

They're all PUZZLES!

I love puzzles. From a young age, I'd pour over Happy Cubes, Nonograms, and grid logic, still thinking about their patterns as I drifted off to sleep.

Around the same time, I fell in love with music. I'd follow this passion along a more linear path.

Fast forward about 15 years, I'm recording albums with my band in New Orleans

The band began acoustic and transitioned to full on synth pop. I taught myself to use synths, MIDI, DAWs, etc in a few months before going live with the new sound.

We toured the US and Canada. I planned routes, booked shows, lined up bands to play with us, and secured lodging.
