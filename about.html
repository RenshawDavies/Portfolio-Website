I traveled the road from indie rocker to data rockstar

Along the way, I’ve sought opportunities to maximize profits and efficiency with the power of data…

My band, Renshaw Davies, booked, promoted, and performed international tours. This took immense organization! I recorded communications, tracked finances, and compared gear purchases using Excel

As an audiovisual technician at the New Orleans Convention Center, we were sometimes short on gear for larger shows. I asked permission to conduct inventory of our storage rooms and make recommendations for future purchases and storage locations based on our needs from previous shows

At mumms Software, I helped to generate leads by researching potential clients in target regions and logging my findings in a Google Sheet

As sales manager at Hollywood Lights, I took the initiative to make projections using our sales history to determine future purchases and pricing

These experiences finally led me to pursue a tech career with Genesis10, where I learned to use SQL as a backend for applications

At Barnet Associates, I've had the opportunity to use Tableau to track payroll and Jitterbit for data integration
